Business Insurance

Business

Running a business isn’t easy so we need to ensure it is well protected against any form of risk. Business might be interrupted by cases of burglary, fire and other natural disaster leading to huge losses. With Biashara insurance you can run a risk free business by protecting your Biashara from such cases through compensation.

The product offers a combined Solution covering stocks and business related tools against such risks as

  • Fire and perils insurance
  • Burglary
  • All Risks -Risks against accidental physical loss or damage to specified items
  • Loss of money by theft and damages to safes or strong rooms
  • Goods on transit
  • Fidelity guarantee
  • Public liability
  • Group personal accident
  • Work Injury Benefit Act (WIBA)
  • Employer’s Liability

The product is designed as a self-rated product, where the customer chooses the different solutions. Political Violence & Terrorism Cover extended up to a limit.
