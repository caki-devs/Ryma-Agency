Property Insurance

This policy provides cover for the insured property against loss or damage caused by:-

  • Fire & explosion from any cause
  • Loss or damage caused by lightening
  • Loss or damage as a result of bush fire
  • Loss or damage caused by earthquake
  • Loss or damage caused by riot or strikes
  • Loss or damage caused by malicious people
  • Flood and water damage
  • Storm damage caused by wind or excessive rain etc
  • Impact by motor vehicle
  • Loss or damage caused by aircraft or any object falling from the air

Points to note on Domestic Package Cover

Terrorism

Loss or damage caused by terrorist is not covered.It can be extended to the policy at an additional premium

Consequential Loss

The policy covers loss of profits as a result of fire.

Burglary

This policy provides cover for loss or damage caused by burglars. Burglary is defined as breaking in or out of premises for the purpose of stealing. Therefore, there must be evidence of use of force to gain entry into or out of the premises. Hold up is covered or use of threat to the employees. However, this is not automatic with some companies and therefore must be requested for specifically. We do this automatically and also include all the essential clauses for our clientâ€™s protection. The cover extends to include damage to the premises caused by burglars. It is therefore important to include cover for plant and machinery for industrial premises. Note: It is also important to extend the policy to include riot and strikes.

All Risks

This policy covers loss or damage to specified items of personal or delicate nature. It includes items such as watches, cameras, computers, calculators, microscopes, typewriters, medical equipment, video cameras, jewellery, television sets, radios, iPads and mobile phones

Claims Process

Remember we walk with you during the claim process.

  • Report the accident to Ryma Insurance Agency immediately
  • Report to the police immediately and obtain a police abstract
  • Complete a claim form giving the replacement cost of lost property
  • Attach a statement from a watchman/guard or any witness

Required Documents

  • Completed Claim Form
  • Original police abstract
  • Replacement quotation
  • Witness statement
  • Adjustment report
